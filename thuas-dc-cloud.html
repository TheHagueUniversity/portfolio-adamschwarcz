Overview

An app that gives an overview of the diverging, converging rules and techniques that you can use during your studies on The Hague's University of Applied Sciences.

Team & Roles

Me @ UX & UI Design

Timeline

September 2019  –  October 2019

Step 1  •  September 2019

Assignment & brand analysis

To start with the assignment I had to look closely into the THUAS brand to hit the spot and comply with its visual language in my design as I chose it for the baseline of the app's branding.

ASSIGNMENT

Design an app that gives an overview of the diverging and converging techniques you use during the Design & Creativity course.

Step 2  •  September 2019

Drafting information hierarchy

Before even touching any prototyping tool I wanted to define all the key functionalities that I expect to include in the app such as resource library, submit a resource, save or like resources, search and others. After that, I drafted a quick information architecture to lead me through my digital design process.

Step 3  •  October 2019

Designing a component library

While I was drawing up initial screens I was also crafty with creating pretty structured components system that helped me to stay consistent throughout every screen. I consider having one source of truth for every component a essential standard for every digital solution.

Step 4  •  October 2019

Delivering a functional prototype

After one month and few days of work and 62 screens in total I got to the stage of quite functional prototype that showcased all necessary functionality and felt very real even though no back-end data was included.
